This application uses your social media account to create a presenter profile. Your social media profile information is not maintained or stored by this application nor is your usage tracked.  A url is maintained during live survey participation in order to send notifications to your device and then it is deleted once the survey is completed.  Again, this information does not contain any information about you or your social media profile. 

This application also does not provide usage information to outside sources such as marketing.  Your presenter profile only contains information that you input and that is not shared with anyone.  This information simply allows use to connect you with your created surveys as a presenter.  Survey results are only maintained for 30 days.  Please download results within that timeframe for your own records.  Thanks again for using Skadoosh.
